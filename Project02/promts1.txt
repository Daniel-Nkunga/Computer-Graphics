USER: The eyes aren't drawing. Do you know what might be going on?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ASSISTANT:

The depth test was the culprit. The eyes and body were all drawn at the same
cz value, making them coplanar. GL_DEPTH_TEST had no way to pick a winner and
the body overwrote the eyes.

Fix applied to ghost.cpp:
- Eyes now drawn at cz + 0.01f instead of cz, nudging them toward the camera
  so the depth test lets them through.

General rule: always give overlapping coplanar geometry a small z offset when
using GL_DEPTH_TEST.
