## Display Primitives
1. GL_POLYGON: fills into series of points
2. GL_LINE_LOOP: connects first and last points
3. GL_LINE_STRIP: does not connect first and last points
4. GL_LINES: connects pairs of points
5. GL_POINTS: draws dots at each point
6. GL_TRIANGLES: draws triangles using pairs of three points
7. GL_TRIANGLE_STRIP: draws triangles using pairs of three points but reusing the last two points from the previous triangle
	1. Ex: (p1, p2, p3), (p2, p3, p4), (p3, p4, p5), . . . 
8. GL_TRIANGLE_FAN: draws triangles using pairs of three points but always using the first point
